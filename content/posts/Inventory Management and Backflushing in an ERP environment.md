## Need of Inventory Management Through an ERP System

- In order to achieve the presentation shown above the company ordinarily needs to
maintain separate accounting and Inventory records. Now, imagine calculating
Weighted Average Value of inventories for the company who’s inventories are
worth Crores of Rupees! It will be difficult.

- Thus ERP systems were introduced which seamlessly integrated these two
functions of the company

- With the help of an ERP the manufacturing companies can now keep the track of
the Inventories, Accounting& Valuation all at the same time!
